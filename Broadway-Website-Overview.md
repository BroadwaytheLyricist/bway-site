## What they actually built

Your site was made with **WordPress + a page-builder tool called Elementor**, plus around **10 extra add-ons** (called plugins) stacked on top to make it work.

Think of it like this: instead of building a house, they bought a **pre-made model home kit** and dropped your photos into it. The design isn't custom — it's a template called a "podcast kit" that anyone can buy and use.

**Why that's a problem:**

- **It's slow.** All those add-ons pile up and drag the site down.
- **It breaks easily.** Every add-on needs constant updates. When one stops playing nice with another, the site can break — and WordPress sites are the #1 target for hackers because of exactly this setup.
- **It costs more to run.** This kind of site needs specific paid hosting and some of those add-ons have yearly fees.
- **You're locked in.** To change anything, you basically need someone who knows that exact tangle of tools.

**The site they built isn't actually finished.**

- **Stock photos that aren't you** — about 150 generic stock images (random radio hosts, headphones, etc.) that came free with the template.

So a good portion of what's there is filler, not your actual content.
